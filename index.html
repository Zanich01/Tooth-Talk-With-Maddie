About Me

Welcome!
My name is Madison Migliore, and I am a Registered Dental Hygienist practicing in Oldham County, Kentucky. I graduated from the University of Louisville School of Dentistry in 2023. My interest in the dental field stemmed from a desire to help others and felt a calling to work in healthcare. Though I initially entered dental hygiene by chance, I soon developed a deep passion for the profession. I am particularly dedicated to educating people on the significance of oral health and its impact on overall well-being. This website aims to raise awareness about dental diseases and offer recommendations for optimal oral care.
